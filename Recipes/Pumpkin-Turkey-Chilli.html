Pumpkin Turkey Chilli

Introduction

Experience the comforting embrace of autumn with our Spicy Pumpkin Turkey Chili. This hearty, flavorful meal is the perfect blend of seasonal ingredients, making it an ideal choice for chilly nights and gatherings with friends and family. With a rich, velvety pumpkin base, lean ground turkey, and a medley of aromatic spices, this recipe is the embodiment of fall in a bowl.

Prep Time: 10 mins
Cook Time: 20 mins
Total Time: 30 minutes
Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ½ cup chopped yellow bell pepper
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 pound ground turkey
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chili powder or more to taste
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 dash salt
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup sour cream

Directions

Step 1

Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion, green bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, and garlic; cook and stir until tender.

Step 2

Stir in turkey and cook until evenly brown; drain.

Step 3

Mix in tomatoes and pumpkin. Season with chili powder, pepper, and salt. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer 15 to 20 minutes. Serve topped with Cheddar cheese and sour cream.
